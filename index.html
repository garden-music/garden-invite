Ok, what is this? Garden is basically a co-op we've built to help collectively grow streaming and listenership at the same time as other artists in your niche. We use daily ads and pool our money to target real listeners. The more people that join, the stronger the results are for everyone.

Who is this for? You probably, if you have this link lol. But mainly this is for folks that already have great music, but not much knowledge or energy for direct response marketing. If you want to grow listenership & associate yourself with other great projects, this is an easy and great way to do so.

HOT TAKES

How does it work? Ads pretty much! We've built up a robust playlist audience that is growing every day. With over 10+ years of marketing experience, we constantly build and test new ads (mostly on meta), which direct people to follow and stream different playlists we've curated across Spotify / Apple Music / Tidal / Youtube Music. However, instead of creating campaigns for each individual artist, we pool the daily adspend budget, creating a multiplier effect for everyone involved.

How much does it cost? $5/day per artist. Billed monthly. Stay as long as you'd like, we just ask that you start with at least 60 days before jumping ship.

Are results guaranteed? Nope! Well sorta. We aren't selling streams. Anything that guarantees a number of streams is basically a scam. This is a marketing collective. HOWEVER, you will see more monthly listeners, while sending better data signals to these streaming platforms. This can up your chances for algorithmic placements, as well as up your vanity metrics for those that care, all while associating yourself more closely with other artists in the collective on the world wide web. We'll even send you performance reports :)
